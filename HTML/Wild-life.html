Best Time For Wildlife

Region Best Trekking Time Ideal Trek Time
Jim Corbett National Park, India November to June Ideal for tiger spotting and safaris during the dry season.
Masai Mara National Reserve, Kenya July to October Best time to witness the Great Migration and predator activity.
Everglades National Park, USA December to April Dry season: Cooler weather and fewer mosquitoes, perfect for wildlife viewing.
South Luangwa National Park, Zambia May to October Dry season: Animals gather near rivers, and walking safaris are popular.
Yellowstone National Park, USA April to May and September to October Wildlife is active during spring and fall with fewer crowds.
Galápagos Islands, Ecuador December to May Warmer season: Great for snorkeling and wildlife like sea lions and iguanas.
